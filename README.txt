GetFinancing (http://www.getfinancing.com/ ) module allows your DrupalCommerce
shop to offer frinancing payments.


Configuration
-------------

This payment module may be added and configured via the normal Rules interface.


GetFinancing account settings
------------------------------

Once installed you only have to fill the account details from your GetFinancing account.
Also remember to add the postback url in your GetFinancing portal to point to {YOUR_SERVER}/cart/getfinancing/complete


Troubleshooting
---------------

For any problems, doubts or questions, please contact us at support@getfinancing.com
